Cindercrown

A 60+ page narrative document for an RPG world created as part of UCF's Writing for Video Games course. Outlines three unique factions, a multi-ending story, characters with complex backstories and motivations, hypothetical gameplay mechanics, and a sample mission sequence. Artifical intelligence was used for concept art, all textual content is my own work with no AI assistance.
