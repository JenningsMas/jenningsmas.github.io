Cindercrown

A 60+ page narrative document for an RPG world created as part of UCF's Writing for Video Games course. Outlines three unique factions, a multi-ending story, characters with complex backstories and motivations, hypothetical gameplay mechanics, and a sample mission sequence. Artifical intelligence was used for concept art, all textual content is my own work with no AI assistance.

Law of The Dragon

A 30 page fantasy comedy script for a short film. The script follows Hyrothadax, a dragon who must fight to retrieve his stolen hoard in the court of law. With a focus on comedy and worldbuilding, Law of The Dragon is an excellent example of my experience developing realistic dialogue and compelling characters.

Excerpt from The Arus Machina

A ~1400 word excerpt from a fictitious religious text created for a group worldbuilding project. Together with 3 of my peers, we each developed in-universe documents which served as background information for a collaboratively developed world, Vides. The Arus Machina excerpt was my contribution to this project. Focused on emulating modern religious texts, this document serves as a “hook”, meant to generate interest in readers about the history and religions of the world of Vides.
