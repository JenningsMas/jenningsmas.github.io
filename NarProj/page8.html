You’re on the trail now. Your Paranormal Detector is blaring, and with a clear visualization of the ghost’s traces in your mind, you can easily track it down, and track it down you do.

You run through the halls of the mansion, chasing faint signatures that register in your magickaly adept mind, when you finally find the ghost floating through the halls.

The spirit looks like a grey-black charcoal drawing of a man made by a kindergartener.

Clearly it came into being recently, more advanced ghosts look much more… real, than this.

Advanced or not, a ghost is still deadly. You stand across the hall from it, and take a fighting stance. It, however, turns and begins to fly away!

Your instincts kick into gear. You could chase the ghost, but that carries some measure of risk, you have no clue what magickal powers it could possess. Or, you could take a magickal approach...
