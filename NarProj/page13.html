A Spectral Crime

Back to Start About the Author Target Audience

You search through the fireplace. You almost dismiss it as nothing before you reach up into the chimney to find a package. A package of, on further investigation, occultism supplies.

There’s nothing illegal inside to your relief (and slight disappointment. A famous wizard not having a stash of illegal occult paraphernalia is quite disappointing) , just standard equipment for a summoning ritual. You yourself know how to use it… and you could use it to summon the ghost you're looking for. Or, if you twist the ritual enough, you could instead locate traces of the ghost. It would be a much more subtle approach, and sometimes the best way to catch a ghost is off guard.
