"A Spectral Crime" is a narrative aimed at a more mature audience. Children under 12 should not read this narrative without parental permission.

"A Spectral Crime" is a modern-fantasy narrative surrounding a deep mystery lying under an unexpected murder. Players must search throug the scene of the crime using both mundane and magical means alike. Readers who enjoy fantasy and mystery stories will find familiar elements in "A Spectral Crime" and will hopefully enjoy this narrative.
