Just like the foyer, the living room is full of clocks, though in a more tasteful manner. You can actually count how many there are this time instead of facing down a comically large collection.

Nothing here strikes you as out of the ordinary on first glance. Just a standard, if extremely opulent, living room. Nice couch, some interesting paintings of Charles Clockworth in his younger years (one of them depicts him riding a horse into battle, which you’re at least 80% sure he never did), and a rug that looks like it’s worth more than your downtown apartment.

Despite the normalcy, your P.D. screams wildly, you don’t even need to check the readings to know there’s definitely something something off in this room.

As you focus in, two things pop out. The logs in the fireplace, still smoldering, appear to be oddly unburnt in the center, and that rug has some odd red stains...
