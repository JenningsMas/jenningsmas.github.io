A Spectral Crime

Back to Start About the Author Target Audience

With a quick incantation and wave of your hand, you cast a spell to seal off the halls. A hazy, glowing white barrier envelops both ends of the hallway, trapping the ghost within.

Unfortunately for you, you’ve just trapped yourself inside a room with a murderous ghost. You curse your excellent spellcasting instincts for defaulting you to a barrier spell. Maybe if you hurled a fireball…

No time to think! The spirit turns, unable to leave, and flies at you with arms outstretched! You feel your magick swell within you, you could cast another spell, but that’s what got you into this situation. Alternatively, you’ve never known a problem that couldn’t be somewhat solved by a good, mundane punch to the face...
